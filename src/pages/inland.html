The Brief

This rapidly growing medium size house builder was struggling to keep customers on their site, due to poor navigation and poor access to information. We solved this problem by building stand-alone sites for each development site, while providing brand continuity through a central website. The navigation is split into 2 separate navigation one top bar in the header and an off canvas side bar. This allowed us to allow users to navigated easily to the most important sites, will providing an uncuttered aesthetic on the website.

With developers now creating small websites for each development, Inland wanted a way of creating bespoke pages for each development, that would act as a stand-alone site.

To make this as easy for Inland Homes to develop individual sites, while maintaining a continuous brand, we make a customisable site, with small changes such as colour distinguishing each site. Subsequently, the site has a large amount of customisation without letting the site break away from its core layout and styling.

Focusing on the user experience has resulted in Inland Homes now having a modern website to showcase and sell their expanding collection of developments.
