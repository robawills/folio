Presland Services

The Brief

As a start up business Presland needed to give the right impression. They wanted a website that provided clear and easy information, making them look reputable and professional. Simultaniously, Presland also wanted an updated brand to launch with the website.

Service
  • Branding
  • UX/UI design

Year

www.preslandservices.com

Branding

The logo and type face were was refreshed and refined. The core look of the logo remained changed, still based on a flame/water droplet, but the icon has been modernised. The font used is proxima nova to give a more refined less standard look and feel. The colours have been chosen to reflect Presland calm approach making their clients feel at ease, and it flows through the site itself using a mixture of white space and blues.

The Journey

Using a one page format, it gives the user freedom to discover more by scrolling through the site, or by linking directly to the section they want, by using the clear simple navigation. Each section is broken down to make it plain and simple for the user. The aim was to make a stress free website that is easy to navigate and find information, making tasks like finding a plumber, an easy task.

The Results

The results is a website that really sets Presland apart from his competitors, giving them a professional online presence that leads to more business. The website is delivering work through increased enquiries, easily available information and a better online presence.
